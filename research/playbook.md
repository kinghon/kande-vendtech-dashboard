
## Vendingpreneurs: 5 Mistakes That Cost Beginners Thousands (2026-03-21)
- **Location before equipment** — professionals reverse the order: measure space → match machine → signed agreement → select product → buy. Equipment never dictates location.
- **Concentrated traffic > raw unit count** — 300-unit high-rise with single lobby beats 700-unit garden complex with scattered buildings. Look for: choke points, waiting areas, line-of-sight visibility, habitual traffic.
- **Run the full financial stack** — $5K/mo gross → subtract 30-40% COGS, card fees, revenue share, fuel, maintenance, insurance = ~$2K net. "Revenue is vanity, profit is sanity, cash flow is reality."
- **Data decides product mix** — test, track sales, cut losers, increase winners, adjust pricing. Personal preference costs money.
- **Systems before scale** — stocking = $20-25/hr task. Location acquisition = $1,000/hr task. If you do the $25/hr work, you cap growth. Scale requires delegation.
- **90-day rule** — not married to locations. Analyze, adjust, or relocate after 90 days. These are movable assets. Operate like an investor.
