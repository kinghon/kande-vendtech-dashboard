How to Use the Vendingpreneurs Glossary
Modern Amenities
⚡️

This glossary is here to help you make sense of the language you’ll hear inside this community.

In vending, people often reference manufacturers, machine models, tools, financing options, and shorthand without stopping to explain them. This space gives you quick, plain-language context so you can follow conversations without feeling behind.

You don’t need to read this from top to bottom. Most members use the glossary as a reference when a term comes up and they want quick context.

On desktop, you can use your browser’s find tool (Command/Ctrl + F) to search within the glossary. On mobile, scroll through the alphabetized sections to find what you’re looking for. You can also use the Space search to explore related terms and content.

How to get the most out of it

Look up terms you hear on calls or see referenced in posts

Skim definitions to understand what something is and where it fits

Follow links when you want deeper context (for example, to VendHub or related spaces)

Some entries include simple labels (like financing, tools, or manufacturers) to help you understand what kind of thing you’re looking at. When relevant, you’ll also see links pointing you to where active partners, tools, or deeper discussions live elsewhere in the community.

This glossary isn’t meant to be exhaustive or static. It will grow as new terms, tools, and references come up — just like the business itself.

If you ever hear something you don’t recognize, this is a good place to start.

If you hear a term that should be added, you can suggest it in the Glossary Requests thread here.

Ambient vs cold

A way to describe whether a product is stored at room temperature (ambient) or in a refrigerated section (cold).

Context: #Machines #Merchandising
Where this shows up: When planning product mix or machine layout

Certificate of insurance (COI)

A document provided by an insurance company that proves a business has active insurance coverage and lists key policy details.

Context: #Operations #Compliance
Where this shows up: When a property requests proof of insurance

COGS

Cost of Goods Sold; the direct cost of purchasing the products that are sold through a machine.

Context: #Finance
Where this shows up: When calculating margins or profitability

Cost

The amount paid to acquire a product, service, or asset, before any markup or profit.

Context: #Finance
Where this shows up: When pricing products or evaluating expenses

CRM

Customer Relationship Management software used to track contacts, conversations, and follow-ups over time.

Context: #Sales #Systems
Where this shows up: When managing outreach or property relationships

E
F


Fit criteria

The set of characteristics used to evaluate whether a location is a good match for vending, such as traffic patterns, layout, and population.

Context: #Sales #Launching
Where this shows up: When filtering or evaluating potential locations

Foot traffic

The amount and pattern of people moving through a space as part of their normal routine.

Context: #Sales #Launching
Where this shows up: When evaluating location quality or placement zones

G


Garden-style layout

A property layout with multiple buildings or entrances spread across a site rather than a single centralized structure.

Context: #Sales #Launching
Where this shows up: When assessing how easily people pass a machine

Gross margin

Gross profit expressed as a percentage of revenue.

Context: #Finance
Where this shows up: When comparing profitability across products

Gross profit

Revenue minus the cost of goods sold.

Context: #Finance
Where this shows up: When evaluating product or machine performance

Gross revenue

Total sales before any costs, fees, or expenses are deducted.

Context: #Finance
Where this shows up: In sales reports and performance summaries

HaHa

A smart vending and micromarket platform offering connected machines and self-checkout technology.

Context: #Machines #Platforms
Where this shows up: When discussing machine or platform options

J

Kit / kitting

The process of grouping multiple products together to stock, transport, or service machines more efficiently.

Context: #Operations
Where this shows up: During picking or route preparation

L


LLC

Limited Liability Company; a business structure that separates personal and business liability.

Context: #Finance #Compliance
Where this shows up: When forming or describing a business entity

M


Margin

The difference between a product’s selling price and its cost, expressed either as dollars or percentage.

Context: #Finance
Where this shows up: When deciding what products to carry

Micromarket

An unattended self-service retail space with open shelving and self-checkout, typically larger than a vending machine.

Context: #Machines #Platforms
Where this shows up: When comparing vending formats

Micromart

A vending machine manufacturer known for smart vending units with large video screens and digital price displays.

Context: #Machines #Platforms
Where this shows up: When comparing smart vending hardware or machine features
