Operator Walkthroughs
Modern Amenities

Feb 15, 2026

These walkthroughs show how real operators handle common situations in their routes — from stocking and cleaning to layout decisions and routines.

Many of what you’ll see here are best practices. They’ve worked well in real conditions. At the same time, they’re individual methods, shaped by location type, operator preference, and stage of growth. There’s rarely just one “right” way to do any of this.

As you watch, pay attention to:

The decisions being made

The tradeoffs being accepted

The systems that simplify repeat work

Different operators may solve the same problem differently. That’s intentional. Over time, patterns emerge — and those patterns are often more useful than copying a single approach exactly.

Use these walkthroughs as reference points and inspiration.
The goal isn’t to standardize your process — it’s to help you build one that fits.

If something stands out to you, feel free to note it in the comments — observations and comparisons are especially useful here.
