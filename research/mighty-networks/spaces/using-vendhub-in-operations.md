Reality Check: VendMarket Setup Takes Time (and That’s Normal)

Before you can place orders through VendMarket, your delivery address has to be verified with our distribution partners.

This exists because:

you’re getting access to commercial distribution

pricing, rebates, and bottler relationships depend on verified routing

once you’re set up, ordering becomes predictable instead of scrappy

What this unlocks:

eligibility for USG rebates

access to bottler pricing (Pepsi live now, Coke coming)

a consistent OpCo relationship instead of one-off sourcing

The key mindset:
This is a one-time setup step that removes friction later.

What To Do First (One-Time Setup)
Step 1: Submit your delivery address

Use the exact address where you want deliveries (not just your business mailing address)

Residential, commercial, or coordinated locations are all valid

Address verification typically takes 7–10 days

Orders can’t be placed until verification is complete

📌 If you haven’t already submitted your address or need help:
Email vendhub@modern-amenities.com with your email + delivery address so onboarding can be verified.

Step 2: Upload tax exemption documents

Upload directly inside VendHub (not to Vistar)

Use the Address section in your account settings

Make sure documents are legible and valid for your state

⚠ Important:
Vistar does not issue retroactive tax refunds.
Submit exemption docs before ordering.

What happens next

Addresses are reviewed weekly

Vistar assigns your account + OpCo

Your OpCo reaches out to confirm a delivery day

Once confirmed, you’re ready to order

If you don’t hear from your OpCo, that’s solvable — not a failure.
(Details in the support section below.)


USG & Bottler Enrollment (Optional Leverage, Not Pressure)

Placing one completed VendMarket order automatically enrolls you in:

USG rebates

bottler account creation (Pepsi now, Coke coming)

Enrollment happens once per quarter only.

2026 enrollment deadlines:

March 6, 2026

June 5, 2026

September 4, 2026

December 4, 2026

If you miss a deadline, enrollment simply rolls to the next quarter. Nothing breaks.

Many operators place an early “foundation” order — even a will-call — so future benefits are already unlocked.

How Operators Actually Use VendHub (Patterns, Not Rules)
Early on (first few machines)

Buy machines through VendEquipment

Set up VendMarket once

Place one order to qualify for USG

Learn how OpCos, delivery days, and case ordering work

Explore VendFinance if needed

Focus here: remove friction, not optimize.

As volume grows

Use VendMarket as a core backbone

Supplement intentionally with other sources

Build real relationships with OpCo reps

Time orders around cadence instead of emergencies

Use VendMarket to access USG + bottler pricing

This is where time savings and margin clarity start to compound.

When scaling becomes real

Use VendFinance for multi-machine growth or upgrades

Use VendLeads selectively when time or bandwidth is tight

Pay attention to VendInsights as shared data improves

Keep VendRoute visible for future acquisitions or exits

Nothing here is mandatory.
Optionality is the feature.

Why Participation Matters (Even If You’re Early)

VendHub works best as shared infrastructure.

The more operators who:

place a VendMarket order

enroll in USG

participate in VendInsights

…the stronger pricing, intelligence, and negotiating power become for everyone.

There’s real power — and peace of mind — in making decisions based on validated patterns instead of anecdotes or guesses.

Need the mechanics?

If you’re stuck on logistics or want exact details:

Use these when you need them — not as homework.
