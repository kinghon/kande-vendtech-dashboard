## Video 1: March 11, 2026 VP Weekly — Jeff Scherdin/Vistar (Guest)
**Transcript:** vp-weekly-march-11-2026-vistar.md

### Key Insights

**Vistar/USG Distribution & Ordering**
- USG members get off-invoice discounts tied to order size (e.g., 2% off orders over $1,500 through Modern Amenities deal)
- "Double dipping": Combine pre-buy seasonal discounts + off-invoice order size discounts simultaneously
- Pre-buys: Must commit in advance for seasonal SKUs (e.g., cake cup flavors) — locks in production quantity
- Off-invoice: Automatic discount applied at bottom of Vistar invoice — no separate action needed
- Tiered discounts: Larger orders = larger % off. Example: Orders just below threshold lose the tier discount — cluster orders to hit threshold

**Demand Forecasting / Getting Priority Fill**
- New MA members are being filled from "safety stock" only — no forecasted demand loaded yet
- To stop being cut on orders: Contact your opco/account executive explicitly and tell them your weekly volume
  - Example: "I need 10 cases of Coke Zero weekly" → they load it into their buying system (Clarity)
- Without this, franchises like Canteen get priority and you're perpetually shorted
- Process: Contact opco → load your demand → they pre-purchase → you stop getting cut

**Soda / Bottler Zip Code Lockouts**
- Coke and Pepsi have zip-code contracts that prevent Vistar from distributing in certain areas
- Solution: Find your local bottler distributor directly — Vistar cannot override this
- Dr. Pepper: Restricted in some markets (e.g., Chicago only get bags, not cans/cases via Pepsi)
- Check with your opco to confirm if you're actually in a locked zone vs just out-of-stock

**Vistar Events (Sales Shows)**
- 3x/year regional shows: Northeast (Atlantic City, Sept), South (Orlando, March), West (Anaheim, June)
- Free to attend but RSVP required — contact opco or Jeff directly
- Deep supplier discounts at shows, great networking — highly recommended
- NAMA show also recommended (Chicago, Wed-Fri) — attend Thursday for less crowded conversations

**Platform Notes (Mighty Networks)**
- Community moved from Skool — replays with summaries/transcripts coming soon
- "Chat" area is for casual convos; main feed for detailed questions to preserve community signal
- "What Would You Do Now?" section: Post with max detail for best community response

**Tech Platform Insights (bonus)**
- SandStar = only true active AI (no human review) — all others (365, MicroMart, HaHa) use passive AI with human backend approval
- HaHa uses huge overseas approval teams to make transactions fast — not true AI
- HaHa testing SandStar-like AI later in 2026 — concern about unsettled transactions without human safety net
- MicroMart: software calls it, human confirms — not really "AI" in a meaningful sense
- Long-term SandStar advantage: lower per-transaction fees since they're not paying army of reviewers

**Kande VendTech Actionable Takeaways**
1. Set up Vistar opco relationship immediately — call and load demand for your top 15-20 SKUs
2. Cluster orders to hit discount thresholds ($1,500+ for 2%)
3. Watch for pre-buy announcements in Mighty Networks — grab seasonal SKUs before they sell out
4. Attend Vistar West Show in Anaheim (June) — free, great deals and networking
5. For SandStar machines: emphasize AI advantage in pitches (no human approval, lower fees)
6. When selling to MSAs: note their Coke/Pepsi contracts may require direct bottler relationships
