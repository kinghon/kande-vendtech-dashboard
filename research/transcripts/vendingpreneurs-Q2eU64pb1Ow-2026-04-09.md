# Pricing Tricks That Boost Vending Sales
Source: https://youtu.be/Q2eU64pb1Ow | Scraped: 2026-04-09

Anchor pricing: expensive Monster next to cheaper Rockstar (higher margin). Use .77 pricing not round numbers. Drinks outsell snacks 70/30. Premium protein shakes (Fairlife) sell at $4.50-$6.00. Don't be afraid to mark up drinks aggressively.
