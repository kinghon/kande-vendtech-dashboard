## Key Insights

- **3-factor location scoring framework: Parking + Availability + Visibility**
  1. **Parking lot full?** Full lot = high foot traffic = high vending revenue. Works universally: medical, hospital, urgent care, pet hospital. If the lot is full, the location is worth pursuing.
  2. **Availability** — Are employees actually physically present? Hybrid/remote workers kill revenue. Key questions: Do they WFH on Mondays? Are they in on Fridays? Is anyone near the break room to see the machine?
  3. **Visibility** — Is your machine/micro market visible and in the path of natural foot traffic?
- **Parking lot is a quick drive-by qualifier** — you can pre-screen locations before ever walking in the door. Full lot = green light to approach.
- **Remote work is a hidden landmine** — a 100-person office doing 3-day hybrid weeks is actually a 60-person location. Factor in physical occupancy, not headcount.
- **Medical/healthcare passes the parking test consistently** — urgent care, pet hospitals, specialty clinics tend to have steady traffic and captive audiences.
- **Kande takeaway:** Build parking lot checks and WFH cadence questions into Jordan's prospecting script. These are fast, low-cost pre-qualifiers that filter bad locations before wasting time on proposals.
