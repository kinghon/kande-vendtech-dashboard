# Why Vending Isn't a Gamble
Source: https://youtu.be/ltKk1X6nKpc | Scraped: 2026-04-09

Location-first approach: qualify high foot traffic, captive audience, no alternatives. If underperforms, move after 90 days. Finance > save — financing lets you earn in 30-60 days vs saving a year for one machine. By time you save for one, could have financed 2-3.
