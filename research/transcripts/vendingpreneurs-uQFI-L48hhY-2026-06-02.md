## Key Insights
1. **HR-backed theft deterrent**: Partner with businesses to post signage stating that theft from the open market is grounds for HR penalty or termination.
2. **Open markets are viable with employer support**: 9 out of 10 businesses will allow theft-deterrent signage when you request it.
3. **Social pressure reduces shrinkage**: Employees are far less likely to steal when their job is explicitly on the line.
4. **Theft prevention is a location negotiation point**: When securing open-market locations, include signage permission as part of the agreement.
5. **Low-cost security**: Signage costs nothing but can dramatically reduce loss rates in honor-system or open-access vending setups.
