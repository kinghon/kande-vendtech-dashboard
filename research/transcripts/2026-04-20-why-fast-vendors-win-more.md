## Key Insights

- **Speed is a differentiator** — property managers are busy and overwhelmed; vendors who respond fast stand out immediately.
- **Commit to a 24-hour proposal timeline** — don't leave it vague. Tell them in the meeting when they'll get the proposal. That's a professional, low-pressure commitment.
- **Set mutual expectations** — frame responsiveness as a two-way thing: "I'll get back to you right away" implies you expect the same, accelerating the decision cycle.
- **Speed shortens the sales cycle** — faster follow-through turns leads into signed agreements quicker, which means money starts sooner.
- **Responsiveness signals reliability** — if you're fast before the deal, they trust you'll be fast with maintenance and service after install.
- **Kande takeaway:** Build a 24-hour proposal commitment into standard pitch language. Fast follow-up should be part of Kande's brand promise — not just a nice-to-have.
