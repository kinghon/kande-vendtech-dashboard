## Key Insights
1. **Promotional products as curiosity drivers**: Discounted OTC meds, ACE bandages, and cough drops advertised on the machine exterior act as "billboards" that stop people in their tracks.
2. **"I thought this was a vending machine" effect**: Unexpected product offerings (health/wellness items) create intrigue and drive people to explore what's inside the smart cooler.
3. **Differentiation in crowded spaces**: In locations with multiple vending options, promoting non-traditional products makes your machine memorable and increases foot traffic.
4. **Promotional pricing as foot-traffic strategy**: The discount isn't just about selling that item — it's about getting people to open the door and browse the full inventory.
5. **Smart machines enable SKU freedom**: Unlike spiral machines with limited slots, smart coolers can carry diverse products — leverage that flexibility with surprising inventory.
6. **Marketing on the machine itself**: The exterior display/promo area is free advertising space — use it to feature something people don't expect from a vending machine.
