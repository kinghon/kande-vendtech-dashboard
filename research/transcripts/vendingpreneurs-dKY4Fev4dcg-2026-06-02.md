## Key Insights
1. **Real estate entry barrier is massive**: $40-50K down payment for only ~$300/month cash flow — thin margins that one repair or bad tenant can wipe out.
2. **Vending as superior asset class**: Compared to real estate, vending machines have lower entry cost, faster cash flow, and portable assets — no tenant risk.
3. **Speaker's personal journey**: Came from real estate background, ran the numbers his whole career, and vending won on risk-adjusted returns.
4. **Liquidity and flexibility**: Unlike real estate, vending machines can be moved if a location underperforms — the asset follows the revenue.
5. **Cash flow density**: Vending generates immediate, daily cash flow vs. monthly rent cycles with vacancy risk.
