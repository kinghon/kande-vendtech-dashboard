## 1. PURPOSE

Kande VendTech will install and operate smart refreshment equipment ("Equipment") at Host's property at **no cost to Host**. Kande VendTech provides the equipment, products, installation, maintenance, and restocking — all at our expense.

This is a service, not a lease. Host is not renting equipment or purchasing products.

---

## 2. TERM AND RENEWAL

**Initial Term:** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **\_\_\_\_** years (**three (3) to five (5) years recommended**).

**Automatic Renewal:** After the Initial Term, this Agreement automatically renews for successive **one (1) year** periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **sixty (60) days** before the end of the then-current term.

**Why the term length?** Kande VendTech invests significantly in equipment, installation, and product inventory for each location. The term length allows us to recoup that investment and provide consistent, quality service.

---

## 3. EQUIPMENT

### 3.1 Ownership
All Equipment placed at Host's property is and remains the sole property of Kande VendTech LLC. Equipment includes, but is not limited to: smart coolers, vending units, payment terminals, cameras, signage, and related technology.

Host shall not move, modify, tamper with, or permit others to move, modify, or tamper with any Equipment.

### 3.2 Placement
Kande VendTech and Host will mutually agree on Equipment placement locations within the property. Placement shall consider foot traffic, electrical access, and visibility.

### 3.3 Utilities
Host shall provide, at Host's expense:
- **Electrical power** — standard 120V outlet(s) within six (6) feet of each Equipment location
- **Wi-Fi access or cellular signal** — sufficient for Equipment connectivity and remote monitoring

Estimated electrical cost per unit is approximately $15-25/month.

---

## 4. PRODUCTS AND PRICING

### 4.1 Product Selection
Kande VendTech retains sole discretion over product selection, mix, and pricing. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate Host's preferences and feedback regarding product offerings.

### 4.2 Product Quality
All products will be fresh, properly stored, and within expiration dates. Kande VendTech maintains proper food handling standards in compliance with the **Southern Nevada Health District** requirements.

### 4.3 Pricing
Kande VendTech sets all product prices. Pricing is competitive with local convenience stores and similar retail outlets.

---

## 5. ACCESS AND RESTOCKING

### 5.1 Access
Host grants Kande VendTech and its authorized representatives **reasonable access** to the Equipment for restocking, maintenance, repair, and removal. This includes:
- Access during normal business hours without prior notice
- After-hours access with reasonable notice for urgent maintenance
- Key, code, or badge access as needed to reach Equipment locations

### 5.2 Restocking Schedule
Kande VendTech will restock Equipment as needed to maintain product availability, typically **one to three (1-3) times per week** depending on sales volume.

### 5.3 Maintenance
Kande VendTech is responsible for all Equipment maintenance, repair, and cleaning at no cost to Host. We aim to respond to service issues within **24 hours** during business days.

## 6. REVENUE SHARE (IF APPLICABLE)

Revenue sharing, if agreed upon, will be documented in a separate **Revenue Share Addendum** attached to this Agreement. Revenue share terms, if any, begin no earlier than **Month 6** of the Agreement.

If no Revenue Share Addendum is attached, no revenue share applies.

## 7. EXCLUSIVITY

### 7.1 Exclusive Rights
During the term of this Agreement, Host grants Kande VendTech the **exclusive right** to provide smart refreshment and vending services at the Location(s). Host shall not:
- Install or permit the installation of competing vending, micro-market, or smart cooler equipment
- Enter into agreements with other refreshment service providers for the Location(s)
- Allow third parties to solicit or provide vending services at the Location(s)

### 7.2 Exceptions
This exclusivity does not apply to:
- On-site cafeterias or restaurants operated by Host or a food service company
- Temporary event catering
- Personal refrigerators or pantries for individual tenant use

## 8. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY

### 8.1 Operator Insurance
Kande VendTech maintains the following insurance coverage:
- **Commercial General Liability:** Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate
- **Product Liability:** Minimum $1,000,000
- **Workers' Compensation:** As required by Nevada law (NRS Chapter 616A-D)

Kande VendTech will provide a Certificate of Insurance upon request and will name Host as an **Additional Insured** upon written request.

### 8.2 Equipment Risk
Kande VendTech assumes the risk of loss or damage to its own Equipment, except where loss or damage is caused by the **negligence or intentional acts** of Host, its employees, agents, tenants, or invitees. In such cases, Host shall be responsible for the cost of repair or replacement.

### 8.3 Indemnification
Each party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other party from claims, damages, or expenses arising from the indemnifying party's negligence or willful misconduct in connection with this Agreement.

## 9. TERMINATION

### 9.1 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement with **thirty (30) days written notice** if the other party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice
- Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases operations
- Engages in illegal activity at or related to the Location

### 9.2 Early Termination by Host
If Host terminates this Agreement before the end of the Initial Term without cause, Host shall pay an early termination fee equal to **$500 per machine** to offset Kande VendTech's unrecovered investment.

### 9.3 Equipment Removal
Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement:
- Kande VendTech shall remove all Equipment within **thirty (30) days**
- Host shall provide reasonable access for removal
- Kande VendTech shall restore the premises to reasonably similar condition, normal wear excepted
- Any revenue share owed through the termination date shall be paid within thirty (30) days

### 9.4 Kande VendTech Right to Remove
Kande VendTech reserves the right to remove Equipment at any time if, in its sole judgment, the Location does not generate sufficient revenue to justify continued service. Kande VendTech will provide **fourteen (14) days written notice** before removal in such cases.
