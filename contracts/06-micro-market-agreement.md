## WHAT IS A MICRO-MARKET?

A micro-market is an **open, self-service refreshment area** — like a small convenience store inside your building. Unlike traditional vending where products are locked behind glass, a micro-market features:

- **Open shelving and coolers** — products are freely accessible
- **Self-checkout kiosk** — customers scan and pay on the honor system
- **Wider product variety** — fresh food, drinks, snacks, and essentials
- **Café-like experience** — more inviting than a vending machine

Because products are openly accessible, this agreement includes specific provisions around **security, shrinkage, and shared responsibility** that differ from a standard vending agreement.

## 5. SECURITY AND SHRINKAGE — THE HONOR SYSTEM

### 5.1 How It Works
Micro-markets operate on an **honor system**. Products are openly accessible, and customers self-checkout at the kiosk. This requires a reasonable level of trust and a shared commitment to maintaining the market.

### 5.2 Security Measures by Kande VendTech
Kande VendTech will install and maintain:
- **Security cameras** covering the micro-market area (footage retained for minimum 30 days)
- **Self-checkout kiosk** with transaction logging
- **Inventory tracking** to identify shrinkage patterns

### 5.3 Security Measures by Host
Host agrees to:
- Locate the micro-market in a **controlled-access area** (not open to the general public)
- Restrict micro-market access to **authorized personnel** only (employees, residents, tenants, and their guests)
- Provide **adequate lighting** in the micro-market area at all times
- **Not disable or obstruct** security cameras installed by Kande VendTech
- Cooperate with Kande VendTech in investigating shrinkage incidents

### 5.4 Shrinkage Allocation
Both parties acknowledge that some shrinkage (theft, spoilage, damage) is inherent in micro-market operations.

- **Normal shrinkage** (up to **5% of gross revenue**) is absorbed by Kande VendTech as a cost of doing business
- **Excessive shrinkage** (above 5% of gross revenue, sustained for two or more consecutive months) triggers the following:
  - Kande VendTech will notify Host in writing
  - Both parties will collaborate on corrective measures (additional cameras, access restrictions, signage, etc.)
  - If excessive shrinkage continues for **three (3) consecutive months** despite corrective efforts, Kande VendTech may, at its option:
    - Convert the micro-market to locked vending equipment (standard agreement terms apply)
    - Reduce the product mix to lower-risk items
    - Terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days notice

### 5.5 Vandalism and Intentional Damage
Host is responsible for the cost of repairing or replacing Equipment damaged by **intentional acts or gross negligence** of Host's employees, tenants, residents, or invitees. Kande VendTech will provide documentation of damage and repair costs.

## 9. FOOD SAFETY AND COMPLIANCE

### 9.1 Health District Compliance
Kande VendTech will operate the micro-market in compliance with all applicable **Southern Nevada Health District** regulations, including:
- Proper food storage temperatures
- Product labeling and dating
- Employee food handler certification

### 9.2 Allergen and Nutritional Information

### 9.3 Recalls
In the event of a product recall, Kande VendTech will remove affected products within **24 hours** of receiving recall notification.

- **Product Liability:** $1,000,000
- **Workers' Compensation:** Per Nevada law (NRS Chapter 616A-D)

Certificate of Insurance and Additional Insured status available upon request.

### 10.2 Host Insurance
Host shall maintain:
- **Commercial General Liability** coverage for the premises
- Coverage should extend to the micro-market area as part of Host's general premises liability

### 10.3 Product Liability
Kande VendTech assumes liability for product quality and safety, provided products have not been tampered with after placement in the micro-market. If products show signs of tampering, Kande VendTech is not liable for resulting claims.

### 10.4 Shared Liability
Because micro-market products are openly accessible:
- **Kande VendTech is responsible for:** Product quality, freshness, proper labeling, equipment function, and food safety compliance
- **Host is responsible for:** Premises safety (slip and fall in market area), access control, lighting, general area maintenance, and cooperating on security
- **Neither party is responsible for:** Voluntary consumption choices made by users, allergic reactions to properly labeled products, or intentional misuse of products

### 10.5 Indemnification
Each party indemnifies the other from claims arising from its own negligence or willful misconduct, including reasonable attorneys' fees.

## 11. TERMINATION

### 11.1 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate with **thirty (30) days written notice** if the other party:
- Materially breaches and fails to cure within thirty (30) days
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Fails to maintain required insurance

### 11.2 Termination for Excessive Shrinkage
Per Section 5.4, Kande VendTech may terminate if excessive shrinkage continues for three consecutive months despite corrective measures.

### 11.3 Early Termination by Host
If Host terminates during the Initial Term without cause, Host shall pay an early termination fee equal to **$1,000 per market location** (higher than standard vending due to greater setup investment).

### 11.4 Equipment Removal
Upon termination:
- Kande VendTech removes all Equipment, fixtures, shelving, kiosks, and cameras within **thirty (30) days**
- Host provides access for removal
- Kande VendTech restores the area to reasonably similar condition (anchor holes, wall mounts, etc. excepted)
- Outstanding revenue share is settled within thirty (30) days

### 11.5 Kande VendTech Right to Remove
Kande VendTech may remove or convert the micro-market if revenue does not justify continued operation, with **thirty (30) days written notice**.
