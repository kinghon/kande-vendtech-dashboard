## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

Kande VendTech will install and operate smart refreshment equipment ("Equipment") across Client's facility or facilities as a **workplace amenity and employee wellness benefit** — at **no cost to Client**.

This is a turnkey service. Kande VendTech provides:
- All equipment, installation, and setup
- Full product inventory and restocking
- All maintenance, cleaning, and technology support
- Dedicated account management
- Custom product selection tailored to your workforce

Client provides space and power. That's it.

---

## 2. TERM AND RENEWAL

**Initial Term:** This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **\_\_\_\_** years (**three (3) to five (5) years recommended**).

**Automatic Renewal:** After the Initial Term, this Agreement automatically renews for successive **one (1) year** periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **ninety (90) days** before the end of the then-current term.

---

## 3. LOCATIONS AND MULTI-SITE DEPLOYMENT

### 3.1 Initial Location(s)
Equipment will initially be placed at the location(s) listed in **Exhibit A** attached to this Agreement.

### 3.2 Additional Locations
Client may request Equipment at additional locations by submitting a written request. Kande VendTech will evaluate each location and, if accepted, deploy Equipment within **thirty (30) days** of approval. Each additional location will be added to **Exhibit A** via a signed amendment.

### 3.3 Deployment Schedule
For multi-location rollouts, Kande VendTech and Client will agree on a deployment schedule. Typical deployment is **one to two (1-2) locations per week**.

### 3.4 Minimum Threshold
Each location must support a workforce of at least **fifty (50) employees** or equivalent daily foot traffic to justify Equipment placement.

---

## 4. EQUIPMENT

### 4.1 Ownership
All Equipment is and remains the sole property of Kande VendTech LLC. Equipment includes smart coolers, vending units, payment systems, cameras, digital displays, signage, and all related technology.

Client shall not move, modify, open, tamper with, or allow others to tamper with any Equipment.

### 4.2 Placement
Kande VendTech and Client will mutually agree on Equipment placement within each facility. Ideal locations include breakrooms, lobbies, cafeterias, and common areas with consistent foot traffic.

### 4.3 Utilities
Client shall provide, at Client's expense:
- **Electrical power** — standard 120V outlet(s) within six (6) feet of each Equipment location
- **Network connectivity** — Wi-Fi access or confirmation of adequate cellular signal for Equipment monitoring

Estimated electrical cost is approximately $15-25 per unit per month.

### 4.4 Technology
All Equipment features:
- Cashless payment (credit, debit, mobile pay)
- Real-time inventory and sales monitoring
- AI-powered product recognition (where applicable)
- Digital display for promotions and messaging (where applicable)

## 5. PRODUCT MIX AND CUSTOMIZATION

### 5.1 Product Selection
Kande VendTech retains final discretion over product selection and pricing. However, we work closely with each Client to tailor the product mix to their workforce.

### 5.2 Custom Product Options
Client may request:
- **Healthy/wellness-focused selections** (low sugar, high protein, organic, gluten-free)
- **Cultural or dietary preferences** (halal, kosher, vegan, etc.)
- **Branded or company-specific items**
- **Seasonal or rotating specialty products**

Kande VendTech will make commercially reasonable efforts to accommodate product requests within our supply chain.

### 5.3 Wellness Program Integration
Kande VendTech supports corporate wellness initiatives by offering:
- **Dedicated healthy option sections** within each unit (minimum 30% healthy items upon request)
- **Nutritional information** displayed on or near Equipment
- **Wellness program tie-ins** — badge or discount codes for employees participating in company health programs
- **Monthly usage reports** showing healthy vs. standard product consumption (upon request)

### 5.4 Employee Feedback
Kande VendTech will provide a feedback mechanism (QR code or digital link) on each unit so employees can request products or report issues directly.

## 6. ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

### 6.1 Dedicated Contact
Client will be assigned a **dedicated account representative** who serves as the single point of contact for:
- Product requests and changes
- Service issues or concerns
- Quarterly business reviews (for accounts with 3+ locations)
- Deployment of additional locations

### 6.2 Response Times
- **Equipment malfunction:** Response within **24 hours** on business days
- **Product request changes:** Implemented within **one (1) restocking cycle** (typically 3-7 days)
- **New location deployment:** Within **thirty (30) days** of approval

### 6.3 Reporting
Kande VendTech will provide Client with:
- **Monthly summary reports** including total transactions, top-selling items, and restocking frequency
- **Quarterly review meetings** (in person or virtual) for accounts with 3+ locations
- **Annual program review** with recommendations for product mix optimization

## 7. ACCESS AND RESTOCKING

### 7.1 Access
Client grants Kande VendTech and its authorized representatives reasonable access to all Equipment locations for restocking, maintenance, and service. This includes:
- Access during normal business hours without prior notice
- Badge, key card, or escort access as required by Client's security policies
- After-hours access for urgent maintenance with reasonable notice

### 7.2 Security Compliance
Kande VendTech personnel will comply with Client's security and safety protocols, including:
- Visitor sign-in procedures
- Background check requirements (if applicable — Client to provide at least 30 days notice)
- Safety training or orientation (if required by Client's industry)
- PPE requirements (if applicable)

### 7.3 Restocking Frequency
Equipment is restocked **one to three (1-3) times per week** based on sales volume and product demand at each location.

## 9. EXCLUSIVITY

### 9.1 Exclusive Rights
During the term of this Agreement, Client grants Kande VendTech the **exclusive right** to provide smart refreshment, vending, and micro-market services at all Location(s) covered by this Agreement. Client shall not:
- Install or permit competing vending, micro-market, or smart cooler equipment at covered Location(s)
- Enter into agreements with other refreshment service providers for covered Location(s)
- Allow solicitation by competing vendors at covered Location(s)

### 9.2 Exceptions
This exclusivity does not apply to:
- Full-service cafeterias, restaurants, or food courts operated by Client or a contracted food service provider
- Temporary catering for events or meetings
- Personal refrigerators or pantry supplies for individual employee use
- Coffee service providers (unless Kande VendTech provides coffee service)

### 9.3 Right of First Refusal
If Client opens a new facility or location, Kande VendTech shall have the **right of first refusal** to provide refreshment services at the new location under the terms of this Agreement.

## 11. TERMINATION

### 11.1 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement with **thirty (30) days written notice** if the other party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within thirty (30) days
- Becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or ceases operations
- Engages in illegal activity at or related to any Location

### 11.2 Termination for Convenience
After the Initial Term, either party may terminate with **ninety (90) days written notice**.

### 11.3 Early Termination by Client
If Client terminates during the Initial Term without cause, Client shall pay an early termination fee equal to **$500 per machine** to offset Kande VendTech's unrecovered investment.

### 11.4 Individual Location Removal
Client may request removal of Equipment from a specific Location with **sixty (60) days written notice**, without terminating the entire Agreement. Exclusivity for that Location ends upon Equipment removal.

### 11.5 Equipment Removal
Upon termination:
- Kande VendTech shall remove all Equipment within **thirty (30) days**
- Client shall provide reasonable access for removal
- Kande VendTech shall restore areas to reasonably similar condition, normal wear excepted

### 11.6 Kande VendTech Right to Remove
Kande VendTech may remove Equipment from any Location that does not generate sufficient revenue, with **fourteen (14) days written notice**.
