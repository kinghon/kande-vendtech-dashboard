## 1. REVENUE SHARE TERMS

### 1.1 Revenue Share Percentage
Kande VendTech agrees to pay Host a revenue share of:

**\_\_\_\_% ( \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ percent)** of **Gross Revenue** generated by Equipment at the Location(s).

*(Standard range: 3% to 5% of Gross Revenue)*

### 1.2 Definition of Gross Revenue
**"Gross Revenue"** means the total dollar amount of all product sales from Equipment at the Location(s), as recorded by the Equipment's payment and inventory systems. Gross Revenue includes:
- All cashless transactions (credit, debit, mobile pay)
- All cash transactions (if applicable)

Gross Revenue does **not** include:
- Sales tax collected (currently 8.375% in Clark County, NV)
- Refunds or chargebacks
- Revenue from test transactions or system errors
- Revenue from Equipment during initial setup and testing (first 48 hours after installation)

### 1.3 Revenue Share Start Date
Revenue share payments begin on the **first day of Month 6** following the Equipment activation date at the Location(s).

**Months 1 through 5** are a ramp-up period during which Kande VendTech is recovering its investment in equipment, installation, initial product inventory, and setup costs. **No revenue share is owed during this period.**

If multiple locations are covered under the Agreement, each location's six-month ramp-up period is calculated independently from its own activation date.

## 2. PAYMENT SCHEDULE

### 2.1 Payment Frequency
Revenue share payments are made **monthly**, within **thirty (30) days** after the end of each calendar month.

### 2.2 Payment Method
Payments will be made via:
- ☐ Check mailed to Host's address on file
- ☐ ACH direct deposit (Host to provide banking information)
- ☐ Other: **\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_**

### 2.3 Minimum Payment Threshold
If the monthly revenue share amount is less than **$25.00**, the amount will carry forward and be included in the next month's payment. Payments are not issued for amounts below $25.00 unless the Agreement terminates, in which case all accrued amounts are paid regardless of threshold.

### 2.4 Payment Accompanying Statement
Each payment will be accompanied by a summary statement showing:
- Total Gross Revenue for the month
- Sales tax excluded
- Refunds/chargebacks deducted
- Net revenue used for calculation
- Revenue share percentage applied
- Revenue share amount paid

## 4. AUDIT RIGHTS

### 4.1 Host Audit Right
Host may, at Host's expense, audit Kande VendTech's records related to Gross Revenue at the Location(s) **once per calendar year**. Audit must be:
- Conducted by a licensed CPA or qualified auditor
- Scheduled with at least **thirty (30) days written notice**
- Limited to records from the prior **twelve (12) months**
- Conducted during normal business hours

### 4.2 Discrepancy Resolution
If an audit reveals that Kande VendTech underpaid revenue share by **more than 5%** in any audited period:
- Kande VendTech shall pay the underpayment within **thirty (30) days**
- Kande VendTech shall reimburse Host for reasonable audit costs
- Kande VendTech shall correct any systemic reporting errors going forward

If the discrepancy is **5% or less**, Kande VendTech shall pay any underpayment within thirty (30) days, but Host bears the audit costs.

## 5. ADJUSTMENTS AND SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

### 5.1 Equipment Downtime
If Equipment at a Location is out of service for more than **five (5) consecutive days** due to mechanical failure (not caused by Host), the revenue share calculation for that month will be adjusted to exclude the downtime period. Kande VendTech will note any downtime periods in the monthly report.

### 5.2 Seasonal Adjustments
Revenue share percentage remains constant regardless of seasonal fluctuations in sales. No adjustments are made for high or low seasons.

### 5.3 Additional Equipment
If Kande VendTech adds Equipment to the Location(s), the new Equipment is automatically included in the revenue share calculation beginning on the first day of **Month 6** after its activation.

### 5.4 Removed Equipment
If Equipment is removed from a Location, the revenue share calculation is adjusted to exclude the removed Equipment starting the month after removal.
