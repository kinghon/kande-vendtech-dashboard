# Kande VendTech LLC — Contract Templates

## Quick Reference: Which Contract to Use

| Situation | Contract | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| Apartment complex or single office building | **Standard Placement Agreement** | Your bread-and-butter. Use for most placements. |
| Large employer (100+ employees), corporate campus | **Corporate/Enterprise Agreement** | Multi-location, wellness language, custom product mix |
| Property management company (Greystar, WestCorp, etc.) | **PM Master Agreement** | One signature covers ALL their properties. The big play. |
| Any deal where you're offering rev share | **Revenue Share Addendum** | Attach to any of the above agreements |
| Prospect who's hesitant or "wants to try first" | **Trial/Pilot Agreement** | 90-day trial, auto-converts to Standard. Gets you in the door. |
| Open shelf/cooler setup (no locked vending) | **Micro-Market Agreement** | Different liability language for honor-system setups |

## When to Use Each

### 1. Standard Placement Agreement (`01-standard-placement-agreement.md`)
**Use for:** Apartment communities (200+ units), office buildings (50+ employees), rec centers, hospitals, warehouses.

This is your default. Covers free placement, maintenance, equipment ownership, exclusivity, and optional revenue share. Start here unless the situation calls for something else.

### 2. Corporate/Enterprise Agreement (`02-corporate-enterprise-agreement.md`)
**Use for:** Large employers (100+ employees), multi-shift facilities, manufacturing plants, corporate campuses.

Adds: multi-location scheduling, custom product mix options, wellness program framing, dedicated account management language. Makes the service feel premium and tailored.

### 3. Property Management Master Agreement (`03-pm-master-agreement.md`)
**Use for:** Property management companies that control multiple buildings (Greystar, WestCorp, Holland, etc.).

One signature = access to all their properties. Includes property onboarding schedule, per-property addenda, and portfolio-level terms. This is how you scale fast.

### 4. Revenue Share Addendum (`04-revenue-share-addendum.md`)
**Use for:** Any deal where revenue sharing is part of the arrangement.

Standalone document that attaches to ANY of the above agreements. Spells out: calculation method, payment schedule, reporting, audit rights. Keep it separate so you can offer rev share selectively.

**Key rule:** Rev share is 3-5% of gross, starting Month 6 — never earlier.

### 5. Trial/Pilot Agreement (`05-trial-pilot-agreement.md`)
**Use for:** Hesitant prospects, new relationship where you need to prove value, competitive situations.

90-day trial with auto-conversion to a full Standard Placement Agreement. Lower perceived commitment gets you in the door. If revenue is under $1,500/month at day 90, you can pull the equipment — no hard feelings.

### 6. Micro-Market Agreement (`06-micro-market-agreement.md`)
**Use for:** Open market setups — unattended shelving, open coolers, honor-system payment. Common in large breakrooms, corporate cafeterias, or co-working spaces.

Different from standard vending because products are accessible without a locked machine. Includes: shrinkage allocation, Host liability for theft/vandalism, camera/security requirements, and different insurance terms.

## Important Notes

- **All contracts are Nevada-specific** — governed by Nevada law, Clark County venue
- **Kande VendTech owns ALL equipment** — this is non-negotiable and stated in every contract
- **Exclusivity is standard** — every agreement includes a non-compete/exclusivity clause
- **Terms are 3-5 years** — protects our equipment investment
- **Rev share starts Month 6** — never earlier, per Skool community best practice
- **These are templates** — have a Nevada attorney review before first use
- **$2,000/month minimum** — if a location can't hit this, don't place there
- **Pull threshold: $800/month** — don't let pride keep a bad machine placed
