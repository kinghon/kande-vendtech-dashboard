## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

This Master Agreement establishes the terms under which Kande VendTech will provide smart refreshment services across **multiple properties** managed by Property Manager — under **one agreement**.

Instead of negotiating a separate agreement for each building, this Master Agreement covers all properties PM authorizes. Individual properties are activated through a simple **Property Activation Form** (Exhibit B) — no need to re-execute the entire agreement each time.

Kande VendTech provides all equipment, products, installation, maintenance, and restocking at **no cost to Property Manager or property owners**.

---

## 2. TERM AND RENEWAL

**Initial Term:** This Master Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for **\_\_\_\_** years (**three (3) to five (5) years recommended**).

**Automatic Renewal:** After the Initial Term, this Agreement automatically renews for successive **two (2) year** periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **ninety (90) days** before the end of the then-current term.

**Individual Property Terms:** Each property activated under this Agreement has its own activation date (per Exhibit B). If this Master Agreement is renewed, all active properties continue. If this Master Agreement terminates, all Property Activation Forms terminate simultaneously.

---

## 3. PROPERTY ACTIVATION AND DEACTIVATION

### 3.1 Adding Properties
To add a property, PM completes and signs a **Property Activation Form** (Exhibit B). Each form specifies:
- Property name and address
- Unit count or employee/traffic count
- Proposed Equipment placement areas
- On-site contact information
- Any property-specific requirements

Kande VendTech will evaluate and, if accepted, deploy Equipment within **thirty (30) days** of receiving the completed form.

### 3.2 Minimum Property Requirements
Each property should meet at least one of these thresholds:
- **150+ residential units** (apartments/condos)
- **75+ employees** on-site daily (commercial)
- **Equivalent foot traffic** that supports projected minimum revenue of $2,000/month

Kande VendTech reserves the right to decline a property that does not meet minimum viability thresholds.

### 3.3 Deactivating Properties
PM may deactivate a property with **sixty (60) days written notice**. Kande VendTech will remove Equipment within thirty (30) days after the notice period. Deactivation of individual properties does not terminate this Master Agreement.

### 3.4 Properties No Longer Under Management
If PM loses management of a property covered by this Agreement:
- PM shall notify Kande VendTech within **fifteen (15) days**
- Kande VendTech may, at its option, negotiate directly with the property owner or new PM
- If no agreement is reached, Kande VendTech will remove Equipment within thirty (30) days

### 3.5 Portfolio Changes
If PM acquires new properties in the Las Vegas/Henderson metropolitan area, Kande VendTech shall have the **right of first refusal** to provide refreshment services at those properties.

---

## 4. EQUIPMENT

### 4.1 Ownership
All Equipment is and remains the sole property of Kande VendTech LLC. Neither PM nor any property owner acquires any ownership interest in the Equipment.

### 4.2 Equipment Types
Kande VendTech will deploy appropriate equipment based on each property's needs:
- **Smart coolers** for apartment common areas and lobbies
- **Full vending units** for high-traffic commercial properties
- **Micro-market setups** for large breakroom/amenity spaces (governed by separate Micro-Market Addendum if applicable)

### 4.3 Utilities
Each property shall provide:
- **Electrical power** — standard 120V outlet(s) within six (6) feet of Equipment
- **Network connectivity** — Wi-Fi access or adequate cellular signal

Utility costs are the responsibility of the property.

### 4.4 Equipment Condition
Kande VendTech will maintain all Equipment in clean, working condition and will repair or replace malfunctioning Equipment promptly.

---

## 5. PRODUCTS AND PRICING

### 5.1 Product Selection
Kande VendTech retains sole discretion over product selection, mix, and pricing. We tailor the product mix to each property's demographics (e.g., family-oriented for apartments, premium options for luxury communities).

### 5.2 Property-Specific Requests
PM or on-site property staff may request specific products or categories. Kande VendTech will make commercially reasonable efforts to accommodate these requests.

### 5.3 Resident/Tenant Amenity
Equipment is positioned as a **property amenity** — a benefit for residents, tenants, or employees. Kande VendTech will provide amenity signage and branding that presents the service as part of the property's offerings.

---

## 6. ACCESS AND OPERATIONS

### 6.1 Access
PM grants Kande VendTech and its authorized representatives reasonable access to Equipment at all active properties for restocking, maintenance, and service. This includes:
- **Lobby, common area, and amenity space access** during business hours without prior notice
- **Key, FOB, gate code, or badge access** as needed to reach Equipment
- **After-hours access** with reasonable notice for urgent service needs

### 6.2 On-Site Coordination
PM shall designate an **on-site contact** at each property (e.g., property manager, maintenance supervisor, leasing manager) for day-to-day coordination. Contact information is captured on the Property Activation Form.

### 6.3 Restocking
Restocking occurs **one to three (1-3) times per week** per property based on demand.

### 6.4 Maintenance and Repairs
Kande VendTech handles all maintenance and repairs at no cost to PM or property. Response time for service issues: **24 hours** on business days.

---

## 7. REVENUE SHARE

### 7.1 Portfolio-Level Revenue Share
If agreed upon, revenue sharing will be documented in a **Revenue Share Addendum** attached to this Agreement. Revenue share may be structured per-property or as a portfolio aggregate.

### 7.2 Start Date
Revenue share begins no earlier than **Month 6** of each property's individual activation date.

### 7.3 Reporting
Kande VendTech will provide PM with monthly reports showing sales activity by property, including units sold, revenue, and top products.

---

## 8. EXCLUSIVITY

### 8.1 Exclusive Rights — Portfolio Wide
During the term of this Agreement, PM agrees to grant Kande VendTech the **exclusive right** to provide smart refreshment and vending services at **all properties activated under this Agreement**. PM shall not:
- Install or permit competing equipment at activated properties
- Enter into agreements with other vending or refreshment service providers for activated properties
- Allow solicitation by competing vendors at activated properties

### 8.2 Preferred Vendor Status
For properties NOT yet activated under this Agreement, PM agrees to present Kande VendTech as a **preferred vendor option** and provide right of first refusal before engaging competitors.

### 8.3 Exceptions
Exclusivity does not apply to:
- On-site restaurants, cafés, or food trucks arranged by the property
- Temporary event catering
- Personal refrigerators in individual tenant spaces

---

## 9. REPRESENTATIONS AND AUTHORITY

### 9.1 PM Authority
Property Manager represents and warrants that it has the **authority to enter into this Agreement** on behalf of the properties it manages, including the authority to:
- Grant access to common areas and amenity spaces
- Authorize equipment installation
- Grant exclusive vending rights at managed properties

### 9.2 Property Owner Notification
PM shall notify property owners of this Agreement as required by PM's management agreements. If a property owner objects to Equipment placement, PM shall notify Kande VendTech within **fifteen (15) days**, and the property will be deactivated per Section 3.3.

---

## 10. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY

### 10.1 Operator Insurance
Kande VendTech maintains:
- **Commercial General Liability:** Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate
- **Product Liability:** Minimum $1,000,000
- **Workers' Compensation:** As required by Nevada law (NRS Chapter 616A-D)
- **Commercial Auto:** As required for service vehicles

Kande VendTech will provide Certificates of Insurance and name PM and/or individual property owners as **Additional Insureds** upon written request.

### 10.2 Equipment Risk
Kande VendTech assumes risk of loss for Equipment, except where caused by **negligence or intentional acts** of PM, property staff, tenants, residents, or invitees. PM shall promptly notify Kande VendTech of any damage to Equipment.

### 10.3 Indemnification
Each party indemnifies the other from claims arising from its own negligence or willful misconduct. This indemnification includes reasonable attorneys' fees and court costs.

---

## 11. TERMINATION

### 11.1 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate with **thirty (30) days written notice** if the other party:
- Materially breaches and fails to cure within thirty (30) days
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Loses the legal authority to perform (e.g., PM loses all management contracts)

### 11.2 Early Termination by PM
If PM terminates this Master Agreement during the Initial Term without cause, PM shall pay an early termination fee equal to **$500 per machine** across all active properties.

### 11.3 Equipment Removal
Upon termination:
- Kande VendTech removes all Equipment from all properties within **thirty (30) days**
- PM provides reasonable access for removal at each property
- Outstanding revenue share payments are settled within **thirty (30) days**

### 11.4 Kande VendTech Right to Remove
Kande VendTech may remove Equipment from any individual property that does not meet minimum revenue thresholds, with **fourteen (14) days written notice** to PM.

### 12.1 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the **State of Nevada**. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of **Clark County, Nevada**.

### 12.2 Entire Agreement
This Master Agreement, including all Exhibits, Property Activation Forms, and Addenda, constitutes the entire agreement.

### 12.3 Amendments
Amendments require written agreement signed by both parties. Property Activation Forms may be executed separately without amending this Master Agreement.

### 12.4 Assignment
Kande VendTech may assign to a successor or business purchaser. PM may not assign without prior written consent, except to a corporate successor entity that assumes all PM obligations.

### 12.5 Confidentiality

### 12.6 Notices
All notices in writing, delivered to addresses above via email with confirmation, certified mail, or hand delivery.

### 12.7 Severability
Unenforceable provisions do not affect remaining provisions.
