## Bug Fixed: Analytics Funnel Showing 0 Proposals

**Problem:** The `/api/analytics/funnel` endpoint was reporting `proposals: 0` even though 28 prospects have status `proposal_sent` and 1 has `negotiating`. The funnel logic only checked for activity type `proposal`/`quote`, which is never logged — proposals are tracked via prospect status, not activities.

**Fix:** Updated funnel calculation to also count prospects with `status=proposal_sent` or `status=negotiating` as reaching the proposals stage.

**Result:**
- Before: `proposals: 0`
- After: `proposals: 29`

**Commit:** `3f5609b` — `[ralph] fix: funnel analytics now counts proposal_sent status prospects as proposals`
