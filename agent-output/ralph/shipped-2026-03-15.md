## Bug Fixed: Route Shadowing — /api/goals/achievements & /api/goals/summary

**Severity:** Medium — goals.html achievements section broken for all users

**Root Cause:**
Express routes are first-match. The wildcard `/api/goals/:id` was defined at line 14704, before the specific `/api/goals/achievements` (14813) and `/api/goals/summary` (14934) routes. Express was matching "achievements" and "summary" as `:id` parameters, returning `{"error":"Goal not found"}` 404 instead of real data.

**Fix:**
Added the two specific sub-routes BEFORE the wildcard at line 14704 (same pattern as the previous `/api/prospects/dedup-check` fix from commit 8ad7d5f).

**Commit:** `d0dc857`
`[ralph] fix: goals/achievements and goals/summary route shadowing by /:id wildcard`

**Verification:**
- Before: `GET /api/goals/achievements` → 404 `{"error":"Goal not found"}`
- After: `GET /api/goals/achievements` → 200 `{"streaks":{"current":0,"best":0,...},"badges":[],...}`
- After: `GET /api/goals/summary` → 200 `{"total":0,"active":0,...}`
