1. **Infrastructure completion ≠ deal closure.** Seven weeks of unbroken QA, 626 prospects, 841 routes — and $0 MRR. The system exists to support a sales motion, not to replace it. Every engineering hour spent optimizing infrastructure beyond its current quality is a substitution for the 20 minutes that actually closes deals.

2. **All In Aviation Pattern: pop-in + same-day email reply = highest-priority override.** Any lead that generates a pop-in and an email reply within 24 hours of each other jumps to #1 priority, regardless of other pipeline signals. Physical and digital engagement simultaneously is the strongest conversion signal in the system.

Stale urgent alerts train humans to ignore the alerts system. Either the leads are acted on, or they're removed from the alerts to preserve signal quality.

Zero calls logged means the system cannot learn which openers work, which objections kill deals, or which verticals convert fastest. One week of logged outcomes = first real data the coaching system has ever received.
