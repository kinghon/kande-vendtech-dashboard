# Piper SEO Style Guide — Based on 5 Live KandeVendTech.com Posts

## Voice & Tone
- First person plural: "we supply", "we have seen", "at Kande VendTech, we..."
- Professional but conversational. Reads like a knowledgeable business owner explaining things to another business owner.
- Confident and authoritative. No hedging ("might", "could potentially"). State things directly.
- NOT salesy or pushy. Informative first, soft sell second.

## Structure (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- **"Report Highlights" section near the top of EVERY post.** 8-10 bullet points with the most impressive statistics. Each bullet starts with • and a bold number/stat. Example:
  •USD 40.6 billion — US fitness industry revenue in 2024.
  •71% of all vending transactions were cashless in 2024.
- **Dedicated statistics sections** organized by topic throughout the post. Each section has bullet-pointed stats with SOURCE NAMES cited inline (e.g., IBISWorld, Cantaloupe, NACS, UNLV CBER).
- **Analysis paragraphs between data sections.** 4-6 sentences each, flowing prose, connecting the data to why it matters. These are NOT bullet points.
- **Every post must have at least 20 specific data points** with named sources. No vague claims. If you can't find a stat, research harder.
- H2 headings for major sections. H3 for subsections.
- Numbered lists ONLY for step-by-step or ranked items (like "7 Benefits").
- NO tables. Convert data to bullet-point stat format or flowing paragraphs.
- Each analysis section should be substantial (150-300 words).
- **Total post length: 3000-5000 words minimum.**

## Company References
- Drop "Kande VendTech" by name 3-5 times per article naturally.
- Reference "At Kande VendTech, we..." at least once.
- Mention "family-owned Las Vegas vending machine company" where natural.

## Internal Links (CRITICAL for SEO)
- Link to other site pages using relative URLs:
  - /services/ — "free placement program" or "full-service vending solutions"
  - /apartment-building-vending-machines/ — apartment content
  - /hotel-vending-machines/ — hotel content
  - /office-vending-machines/ — office content
  - /combo-vending-machines/ — combo machine mentions
  - /healthy-vending-machines/ — healthy option mentions
  - /meal-vending-machines/ — meal/food mentions
  - /coffee-vending-machines/ — coffee mentions
  - /about/ — company mentions
  - /contact/ — CTA links
  - /blog/other-post-slug/ — cross-link to other blog posts
- Aim for 5-8 internal links per article, woven naturally into sentences.

## Las Vegas Specificity
- Reference real Las Vegas locations, neighborhoods, temperatures, demographics.
- Mention the Strip, Henderson, Summerlin, North Las Vegas, the 15 freeway, etc.
- Reference 110+ degree summers, 24/7 workforce, tourism industry, 40M+ annual visitors.
- Reference swing shifts, casino workers, construction, warehouse/logistics growth.

## SEO Elements
- Title tag: primary keyword + location + year if relevant
- Meta description: 150-160 chars, includes primary keyword and Kande VendTech
- H1: matches or closely mirrors title tag
- Canonical URL: /blog/[slug]/
- Schema markup (JSON-LD) for BlogPosting

## Anti-AI Writing Rules
- NEVER use em dashes (—), en dashes (–), or double hyphens (--). Use commas, periods, or "and" instead. This applies EVERYWHERE including the Report Highlights section and Sources section. ZERO tolerance.
- NEVER use "Here's the thing" / "Let's be honest" / "Let's dive in"
- NEVER use "In today's world" / "In an era of" / "In the ever-evolving"
- NEVER use "game-changer" / "cutting-edge" / "revolutionary" / "seamless"
- NEVER use "navigate" / "leverage" / "landscape" / "robust" / "streamline"
- NEVER use "Whether you're X or Y" pattern
- NO bolded keyword starts on list items (like "**Keyword:** explanation")
- Write like the SEO guy writes — full sentences, detailed paragraphs, authority.

## Sources Section (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- Every post MUST end with a dedicated **"Sources"** section before the footer.
- Numbered list of every source cited in the article.
- Format: `1. Source Name — Report/Article Title Year`
- Example:
  1. Grand View Research — U.S. Retail Vending Machine Market Industry Report 2025-2033
  2. IBISWorld — Vending Machine Operators in the US Industry Analysis 2025
  3. Cantaloupe, Inc. — Micropayment Trends Report 2025
- Minimum 10-15 sources per post. The live posts have 25+.
- Sources must be REAL. Do not fabricate source names or report titles.
- This section builds SEO authority and reader trust. Never skip it.

After DeepSeek generates a post, run this cleanup:
1. Replace all em dashes, en dashes, double hyphens with commas/periods
2. Verify "Kande VendTech" appears 3-5 times. If missing, inject into opening paragraph ("At Kande VendTech, we..."), mid-article, and CTA.
3. Verify internal links exist (minimum 5). If missing, find natural phrases and link them.
4. Verify Report Highlights section exists with 8+ stats
5. Verify Sources section exists with 15+ numbered sources
6. Verify CTA paragraph mentions (725) 228-8822 and links to /contact/

## CTA Pattern
- Soft CTAs woven into content: "To learn more about our free placement program, visit our [services page](/services/)."
- End of article: clear next step with link to /contact/ or phone number (725) 228-8822.
- NOT aggressive "Call now!" — more like "If you are considering vending for your property, we would love to walk through the options with you."
