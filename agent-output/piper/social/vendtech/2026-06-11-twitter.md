## Tweet 2 of 3

With $10K in vending, you can start with 2–3 machines.

A single well-placed machine brings in $1,500–$3,000/month in revenue. At 50% margins and after a $150/mo machine payment, you're looking at $700–$800/month net — per machine.

200%+ cash-on-cash return in Year 1 if you pick the right locations.
