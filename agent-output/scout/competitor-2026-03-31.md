## Industry News (Last 7 Days)

### Connected Vending Hits 8M Units Globally
Berg Insight reports **8.1 million connected vending machines** in operation globally (2025), projected to reach 11.7M by 2030. Cashless payment and "grab-and-go" micro-market formats are the main growth drivers. *(IoT Business News, Mar 27)*

**Relevance:** Validates Kande's AI smart vending positioning. Cashless-first machines are becoming the expectation, not a differentiator — the differentiator is now service quality, product flexibility, and local relationships.

### AI Robotic Coffee Machines Gaining Traction
Press release from Shenzhen-based manufacturer touting AI robotic coffee vending profitability for 2026. Emphasis on 24/7 unmanned retail and choosing the right technology/location/model. *(NatLawReview, Mar 27)*

**Relevance:** More entrants in the "smart vending" space nationally, but these are primarily coffee-specific. Kande's broader food/snack/drink offering remains a wider value prop.

**Relevance:** Low direct impact, but a reminder that tech-lagging operators get left behind. Good talking point for prospects stuck with old-school vendors.
