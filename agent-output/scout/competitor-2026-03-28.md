## 🏢 Competitor Intel: Las Vegas Vending Companies

### Canteen Vending (Las Vegas)
- **Location:** 1509 Western Avenue, Las Vegas, NV 89102 | (702) 387-8789
- **Known weaknesses (from Reddit r/vending community, Oct 2024 + June 2025):**
  - Limited product selection due to bottler-owned machine restrictions
  - Predominantly focused on huge accounts (airports, government) — smaller accounts get neglected
  - Accounts routinely willing to switch vendors — Reddit operators actively follow Canteen trucks to poach accounts
  - Overcharging complaints; money taken with no product dispensed; poor refund responsiveness
  - Product mix doesn't match building demographics at some sites
- **Yelp sentiment:** "They're thieves. Don't use their machines. Customer service is a joke." (pattern repeated across locations)
- **Opportunity:** Canteen's size is their weakness. Smaller local accounts are underserved — prime targets for Kande's personal service pitch.
