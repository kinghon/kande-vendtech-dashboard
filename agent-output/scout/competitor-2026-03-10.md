## 🏆 Competitor Reviews & Intelligence

### Canteen (Las Vegas — 1509 Western Ave, (702) 387-8789)
**Overall Yelp Rating: 1.4/5 (111 reviews nationally)**

Key weaknesses surfaced in public reviews and industry commentary:
- **Broken machines / no refunds:** Top complaint — customers lose money and can't get it back
- **Limited product selection:** Uses bottler-owned machines with restrictions on what can be stocked
- **Long contracts:** Clients get locked in with unfavorable terms
- **Fresh food spoilage:** Struggles to maintain fresh items — a recurring management problem
- **Small accounts neglected:** Locations with lower volume get deprioritized, inconsistent service
- **Charges location fees:** Clients are billed just to have their service — unusual model
- **High prices:** Premium pricing without premium service
- Las Vegas location confirmed active: serves casinos, hotels, warehouses, call centers around the clock
- **Opportunity:** Any site currently with Canteen and experiencing service issues is a warm lead. Their 1.4/5 Yelp score is a door-opener.
