## 📰 Industry News — Last 7 Days

### 1. Regulation Creep: 5 Issues Operators Must Watch in 2026
*VendingTimes, Feb 13, 2026*

The issues most relevant to Kande:
- **Cash-acceptance mandates:** NYC, SF laws already in force requiring cash acceptance. Federal Payment Choice Act of 2025 pending — could require vending machines to accept cash up to $500 transactions. Cashless-only machines may face compliance requirements at public-sector locations.
- **Swipe-fee legislation (Credit Card Competition Act):** Would require large banks to enable routing competition for credit cards. Supporters: lower processing fees for merchants/operators. Still pending.
- **Nutrition labeling / calorie disclosure:** Expanding to vending in some states. Already required in some venues. May require display of calorie counts per item.
- **Plastic/packaging restrictions:** Several states/municipalities restricting single-use plastics — could affect product mix.

**Kande relevance:** Cash-acceptance legislation matters if pitching any school, government, or public-sector facilities. File for future monitoring — no immediate Nevada impact yet.

### 2. Vending Machine Market: 6.6% CAGR Through 2035
*Industry research, week of Feb 17-23, 2026*

- Global vending machine industry projected to reach $45.9B by 2035 at 6.6% CAGR.
- Reverse vending machine segment growing faster (recycling/return machines): 5.52% CAGR.
- Industrial vending (tool/PPE dispensing in warehouses): "Experiencing rapid advancement" — growth sector.
- **Implication:** Industrial/warehouse vending (tool dispensing) is a separate from break-room/food vending. Not Kande's market, but a trend to be aware of as it may confuse prospective clients who've heard of "industrial vending."
