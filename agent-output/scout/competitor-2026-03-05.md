## 1. Industry News (Last 7 Days)

### 🔥 Key Takeaways

**Market Growth — Intelligent Vending on a Tear**
- The global intelligent vending machine market is projected to grow from **$17.7B in 2026 → $53.2B by 2036** (CAGR: 11.6%). AI + IoT are the primary drivers. *(openpr.com, ~1 week ago)*
- Micro markets specifically growing at **~30% annually** — the fastest sub-segment in the industry.
- Smart fridges with weight-based inventory tracking are reducing spoilage/restocking costs for operators. Airports account for 34.7% of market share in 2026.

**Product Trends — What's Selling**
- Zero-calorie drinks, high-protein snacks are top sellers in 2026 per Pyramid Technologies.
- **"Functional Water" and "Clean Energy" sectors up 22% YoY** in vending pull-rates (NielsenIQ early 2026 data).
- Healthy chef-prepared frozen meals continue to outperform traditional snacks in high-end placements — directly relevant to KandeVendTech's differentiator.

**Industry Philosophy Shift — "Right Fit Vending"**
- 2026 trend: growth is no longer about pushing more machines into more places. It's about **smarter placement and tighter alignment** between location type and product mix. Naturals2Go article (1 day ago) validates Kande's approach.
- Strongest operators in 2026 won't be defined by a single machine type — hybrid snack/frozen/healthy mix is winning.

**Workplace Vending Boom**
- Companies are increasingly installing vending as part of **employee wellness programs** — a strong angle for B2B pitches to office complexes and corporate parks.

**NAMA Show 2026 — Coming Up**
- The NAMA Show (the industry's premier event) is scheduled for **April 22–24, 2026 in Los Angeles**. Good opportunity for KandeVendTech to attend and stay ahead of tech trends.

**NBVA Conference — Las Vegas**
