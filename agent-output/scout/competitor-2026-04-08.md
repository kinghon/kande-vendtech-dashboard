## 🔍 Competitor Intel: Las Vegas Market

### Canteen (Compass Group) — Las Vegas
**Location:** 1509 Western Avenue, Las Vegas, NV 89102 | (702) 387-8789
- **Major negative:** Yelp reviews report **machines empty since December 2025** with no response to refill requests despite monthly calls and emails
- **$6.9M Class Action Settlement** (Jilek v. Compass Group) — payouts of $30-$360 per claimant for overcharging on vending machine purchases (2014-2025). Settlement approved, payments going out now via check/PayPal/Venmo/Zelle
- Services casinos, hotels, warehouses, taverns, call centers in LV, Henderson, Boulder City, Sandy Valley
- **Five Star bought their operation** (per Reddit r/vending, Oct 2024) — so Canteen's local franchise may now operate under Five Star
- **Vulnerability:** Empty machines + settlement = damaged trust. Locations may be looking for alternatives.
