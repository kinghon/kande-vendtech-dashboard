## 🎯 Competitor Intel: Las Vegas

### Canteen (Compass Group) — The Incumbent Giant
- **Address:** 1509 Western Ave, Las Vegas, NV 89102
- **Coverage:** Casinos, hotels, warehouses, taverns, call centers. Serves LV, North LV, Henderson, Boulder City, Sandy Valley.
- **NEW — $6.9M Class Action Settlement (March 2026):**
  - Jilek vs. Compass Group USA settlement. Consumers who used Canteen machines between 2014–July 2025 eligible for $30–$360 payouts.
  - **This is a BIG vulnerability.** Class action = reputational damage + operational distraction.
- **Review Complaints:**
  - Yelp: "Machines have been empty since December 2025, no response to getting them refilled, emailed and called every month, nothing."
  - Indeed (employee reviews): "Not organized enough, not enough help. If management didn't care neither did the employees."
- **Takeaway:** Canteen is bleeding on service quality. Empty machines for 4+ months with no response = locations ready to switch. The class action makes them even more vulnerable. **Priority prospecting target: Canteen accounts.**

### Skytop Vending — The Canteen Sub-Brand
